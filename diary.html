    How to use this diary

    1. Morning: Open the diary, fill in Morning Plan with your intention for the day.
    2. Throughout the day: No need to revisit — just live the day.
    3. Evening: Tick the practices you did, fill in reflection, save the entry.
    4. Weekly & monthly: Visit Reports to see how you're tracking.
    5. Anytime: Share your entries with Claude for deeper analysis and gentle advice.
