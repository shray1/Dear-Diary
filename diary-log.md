>

---

## The Vision

**A Good Day (1440 minutes)** spent across:
Adequate Rest · Healthy Meals · Physical Activity · Leisure Time · Social Interaction · Productive Work · New Learnings · Financial Growth · Family Bonding · Spiritual Growth

**Daily intentions:**
- Wake before sunrise
- Morning walk or jogging
- Healthy home-made meals at proper times (body discipline, body clock)
- Morning & evening prayers (commute time on busy weekdays is acceptable)
- Help someone — money, donation, or hands-on help
- No anger throughout the day
- Bonus: made someone happy
- Speak less — preserve energy
- Time with grandma, parents, brother, wife
- Do good · hear good · speak good · watch good
- Apologize for mistakes · Be grateful · Forgive others

**A Good Week:**
- Visit one relative or friend
- 2 high-intensity workouts
- 1 food fast

**A Good Month:**
- Visit Ashram / Satsang / Satguru — 2 times
